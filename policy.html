Privacy Policy

At Playa Pharmacy, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our patients and website visitors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our pharmacy services.

Our pharmacy complies with applicable federal and state laws regarding patient privacy, including the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) and the Health Information Technology for Economic and Clinical Health Act (HITECH).

By using our website, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

We may collect both personal information and protected health information (PHI) when you interact with our website or services.

Personal Information

You may voluntarily provide personal information when you:

  • Contact us through our website
  • Request information about our compounding services
  • Submit prescription inquiries
  • Schedule consultations or request medication information

This information may include:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Mailing address
  • Date of birth (if required for pharmacy services)

Protected Health Information (PHI)

When necessary for pharmacy services, we may collect Protected Health Information (PHI) as defined under Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA).

PHI may include:

  • Prescription information
  • Medical conditions related to treatment
  • Physician or healthcare provider information
  • Medication history

This information is used strictly to provide pharmacy services and maintain patient care coordination.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for purposes including:

  • Processing prescription and compounding service requests
  • Communicating with healthcare providers
  • Responding to patient inquiries
  • Providing customer support and pharmacy services
  • Improving our website and services
  • Complying with legal and regulatory obligations

We only use patient information when necessary to provide safe and effective pharmacy care.

HIPAA Compliance and Protection of Health Information

Our pharmacy implements administrative, physical, and technical safeguards to protect patient information in accordance with Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) requirements.

These safeguards may include:

  • Secure pharmacy systems and electronic records
  • Access controls and staff training
  • Secure communication channels
  • Confidential handling of prescription information

Despite these protections, no internet transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Disclosure of Information

We do not sell or rent patient information to third parties.

We may disclose information when necessary to:

  • Communicate with prescribing healthcare providers
  • Process prescriptions or compounding requests
  • Comply with federal or state legal requirements
  • Protect the health and safety of patients
  • Work with trusted service providers who assist in pharmacy operations

Any third parties with access to patient information must comply with applicable privacy laws and confidentiality requirements.

Cookies and Website Analytics

Our website may use cookies and analytics tools to understand website usage and improve user experience.

Cookies may collect information such as:

  • Browser type
  • Pages visited
  • Website activity

These tools help us improve website performance and service delivery.

You can disable cookies through your browser settings if preferred.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party sites. Users are encouraged to review the privacy policies of those websites before providing personal information.

Your Rights Under HIPAA

Under the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA), patients have certain rights regarding their health information, including the right to:

  • Request access to their health records
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Request restrictions on certain uses or disclosures
  • Receive a record of disclosures of their information

Requests regarding patient information can be submitted using the contact information below.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our services, legal requirements, or privacy practices. Updates will be posted on this page with the revised date.
