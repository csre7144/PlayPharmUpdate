Prescriptions & Delivery

Your Prescriptions, Done Right

At a chain pharmacy, you're a number in a queue. At Playa Pharmacy, we know your name, your medications, and your history. Every prescription gets our full attention — filled quickly, checked carefully, and ready when you need it. We accept most major insurance plans including Medicare Part D, and we'll fight for your coverage so you don't have to.

Fast, Accurate Prescription Fills

We fill prescriptions with speed and precision — but never at the expense of care. Every script receives a thorough review for interactions, duplicate therapies, and dosing concerns before it leaves our hands. New prescriptions are typically ready within 15–20 minutes for in-store pickup. We accept prescriptions by fax, phone, e-prescribe, and in person.

Free Delivery Across California

Your medication comes to you — at no extra cost. We offer free same-day and next-day local delivery throughout the Westside, and free shipping anywhere in California via mail order. Same price as pickup, without the pickup hassle. Whether you're in Playa del Rey, Santa Monica, Westchester, Marina del Rey, or anywhere in the state — we deliver.

Medication Synchronization

If you're picking up medications three different times a month, something's wrong — and it's not you. Our medication synchronization service aligns all your refills so everything is ready together, on one day, on your schedule. One pickup. One delivery. One less thing to manage.

We also send text and call reminders before each refill so nothing slips through the cracks and you're never caught without your medication.

Seamless Prescription Transfers

Switching from another pharmacy? We make it effortless. Give us the name of your current pharmacy, your date of birth, and the medications you'd like transferred. We handle the rest — the phone calls, the paperwork, the coordination. No charge, no hassle. call us at +1 (310) 823-4500.

Insurance Navigation

Insurance should work for you, not against you. Our team handles prior authorizations, investigates copay savings programs, navigates insurance changes, and fights denials when your medication should be covered. If there's a manufacturer coupon, a patient assistance program, or a lower-cost alternative that works just as well — we'll find it. You shouldn't need a degree in insurance to get your prescriptions filled.

Special Orders & Hard-to-Find Medications

When a medication is discontinued, backordered, or simply hard to find, most pharmacies shrug. We don't. We source special orders from our distributor network and work to get hard-to-find medications into your hands as quickly as possible. If it exists, we'll do everything we can to find it.
