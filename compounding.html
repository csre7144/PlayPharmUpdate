Compounding

What is Compounding?

Pharmaceutical compounding is the art and science of customizing medications to meet the specific needs of individual patients. Unlike standard, commercially available drugs, compounded medications are prepared by licensed pharmacists to provide personalized doses, unique formulations, or alternative delivery methods.

Why would someone need a compounded medication?

Compounding is ideal for patients who need a solution that standard medications can’t provide. Examples include:

  • Unique dosage strengths not available commercially
  • Alternative forms (creams, gels, liquids, or troches) for easier administration
  • Allergy-friendly formulations free from dyes, preservatives, or gluten
  • Discontinued or unavailable medications
  • Specialized treatments for children, seniors, or pets

What types of compounded medications are available?

Compounded medications can be customized in many forms, including:

  • Topical creams and gels for dermatology or pain relief
  • Oral capsules or liquids for precise dosing
  • Sublingual troches or lozenges for faster absorption
  • Suppositories for GI or hormone therapies
  • Specialty formulations tailored to hormone therapy, wound care, pediatric care, or chronic conditions

Is compounding safe?

Yes. Compounded medications are prepared in strictly controlled environments using high-quality, FDA-compliant ingredients. Our pharmacists follow USP standards and best practices to ensure safety, consistency, and effectiveness for every patient.

Who benefits from compounded medications?

Compounding can help a wide range of patients, including:

  • Children needing flavored or liquid medications
  • Adults requiring customized hormone therapy
  • Patients with allergies or sensitivities
  • Individuals with chronic or hard-to-treat conditions
  • Those who need medications in a non-standard form or dose

How do I get a compounded medication?

All compounded medications require a prescription from a licensed healthcare provider. Our pharmacists work closely with your doctor to create a personalized medication that meets your specific needs, ensuring maximum safety and effectiveness.

Why choose a compounding pharmacy?

Compounding allows for true personalized medicine, giving patients options that aren’t available in standard pharmacies. With compounding:

  • Every medication is tailored to the individual
  • Adults requiring customized hormone therapy
  • Ingredients are custom-selected for safety and tolerance
  • Dosages and delivery forms are designed for optimal results
