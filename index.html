How to Join Us?

Step 1

Should you receive an order for prescription medication after your next office visit, simply inform your physician that you would like it filled at Playa Pharmacy.

Step 2

You can also have existing prescriptions currently filled at another pharmacy transferred to us by request at no additional charge. Let us know and we’ll make it so!

Step 3

Give us a call by phone or visit in-person to learn more about Playa Pharmacy. We’re open Mondays through Fridays, 9am-6.30pm; Saturdays, 10am-3pm; closed Sundays.
