Pet Medicine

If you've ever tried to get a pill down a cat's throat or watched your dog spit out a tablet wrapped in cheese, you know the challenge. Veterinary compounding turns your pet's medication into a form they'll actually take — a flavored treat, a tiny topical gel applied to the ear, or a liquid mixed into their food.

Playa Pharmacy works with veterinarians across the Westside to compound medications for dogs, cats, birds, and other animals in species-appropriate forms, flavors, and dosages.

What We Compound

  • Flavored oral suspensions and treats — beef, chicken, liver, tuna, and other pet-friendly flavors
  • Topical ear gels for cats — applied to the inner ear for medication without the struggle
  • Custom-strength thyroid medications (methimazole for cats is one of our most common veterinary compounds)
  • Pain management compounds for aging pets
  • Antifungal and antibiotic formulations in veterinary-appropriate forms
  • Behavioral medications (fluoxetine, gabapentin) in easy-to-administer forms
  • Medications that have been discontinued by veterinary manufacturers

Available Forms

  • Flavored liquids and suspensions
  • Topical gels (ear application)
  • Flavored soft chews and treats
  • Tiny capsules for small animals

For pet owners: If your pet won't take their medication, talk to your veterinarian about a compounded alternative. You can also call us directly at +1 (310) 823-4500 and we'll coordinate with your vet.
