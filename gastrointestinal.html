Gastrointestinal Health

Digestive conditions can be complex and deeply individual. When standard medications cause intolerable side effects, come in the wrong form, or contain ingredients a patient can't tolerate, compounding provides an alternative. We prepare GI medications in forms and formulations that work with your body — not against it.

What We Compound

  • Low-dose naltrexone (LDN) for inflammatory bowel conditions including Crohn's disease
  • Acid-resistant capsules for medications that must survive the stomach
  • Custom-strength oral suspensions for patients who cannot swallow pills
  • Allergen-free formulations — free of gluten, dye, lactose, or other triggers
  • Combination preparations to reduce pill burden for patients on multiple GI medications
  • Anti-nausea preparations in alternative delivery forms

Available Forms

  • Oral capsules and suspensions
  • Sublingual troches
  • Suppositories
  • Allergen-free and sensitivity-friendly formulations
