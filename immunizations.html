Immunizations

Walk In. Roll Up Your Sleeve. Done.

Protecting your health shouldn't require a scheduling marathon. At Playa Pharmacy, we offer a wide range of CDC-recommended vaccines with no appointment necessary. Walk in during business hours, let our pharmacists take care of you, and walk out with peace of mind. Whether it's flu season, time for a booster, or you're preparing for travel — we make it effortless.

Vaccines We Offer

Our pharmacists are certified to administer a full range of vaccinations for adults and eligible children, including:

  • Influenza (Flu) — seasonal flu shots, including high-dose for seniors
  • COVID-19 — primary series and updated boosters
  • Shingles (Shingrix) — recommended for adults 50+
  • Pneumonia (Prevnar 20, Pneumovax) — recommended for adults 65+ and at-risk populations
  • Tdap / Td — tetanus, diphtheria, and pertussis boosters
  • Hepatitis A & B
  • MMR — measles, mumps, and rubella
  • Varicella — chickenpox vaccine
  • Meningococcal
  • RSV — for eligible adults 60+
  • Travel vaccines — ask our pharmacist about recommended vaccinations for your destination

How It Works

  • No appointment needed. Walk in during business hours — Monday through Friday, 9am–6:30pm; Saturday, 10am–3pm.
  • Most insurance accepted. We bill your insurance directly. Many vaccines are covered at no cost under preventive care benefits.
  • Quick and comfortable. Most visits take 15 minutes or less, including the required observation period.
  • Records provided. We document your vaccination and can provide records for your primary care provider, employer, school, or travel requirements.

Not Sure What You Need?

Our pharmacists can review your vaccination history and recommend which immunizations you may be due for based on your age, health conditions, and CDC guidelines. Just ask — it's part of the service, and there's no charge for the consultation.
