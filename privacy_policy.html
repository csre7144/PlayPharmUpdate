Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2026

Overview

Playa Pharmacy is committed to protecting the privacy of our patients and website visitors. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our pharmacy services. Our pharmacy complies with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA), the Health Information Technology for Economic and Clinical Health Act (HITECH), and applicable California state privacy laws.

Information We Collect

Personal Information: When you contact us through our website, submit prescription inquiries, or request information about our services, you may voluntarily provide your name, email address, phone number, and mailing address.

Protected Health Information (PHI): When necessary for pharmacy services, we may collect PHI including prescription information, medication history, and healthcare provider information.

How We Use Your Information

We use information we collect to process prescriptions and compounding requests, coordinate care with your healthcare providers, respond to inquiries, and comply with legal and regulatory obligations. We use PHI only as necessary to provide pharmacy services.

How We Protect Your Information

We implement administrative, physical, and technical safeguards to protect your information in accordance with HIPAA requirements. These include secure pharmacy systems, access controls, staff training, and secure communication channels.

Disclosure of Information

We do not sell your personal information or PHI. We may disclose information as necessary to coordinate care with your prescribing healthcare providers, process prescriptions and insurance claims, comply with federal or state legal requirements, and work with trusted service providers bound by confidentiality obligations.

Cookies and Website Analytics

Our website may use cookies and analytics tools to understand website usage and improve your experience. You can disable cookies through your browser settings.

Third-Party Links

Our website may link to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
